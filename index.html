About

Dr. Jakdej "Jak" Nikomborirak MD is a sleep doctor with over twenty years of experience, In this time he has helped patients overcome various sleep disorders such including snoring, sleep apnea, narcolepsy. Supplementing his profession with his background in aviation, he now flies all over Washington to reach as many people as possible. You can contact him at drjak@soundsleepclinic.com or find him at one of many work locations.
